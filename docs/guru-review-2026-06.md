## Fixed in this pass

1. **Malformed `summary` frontmatter (systemic).** The content-generation
   workflow's auto-summary had captured truncated fragments / leaked headings /
   a broken link on ~20 lessons. **All 30 workflow-generated summaries were
   replaced with hand-authored one-sentence summaries.** (The 2 pilots + ICT
   were already clean.)
2. **Minervini — return figure.** Removed the internally inconsistent
   "~33,500% total return" gloss; kept the sourced "~220%/yr over 5.5 years."
3. **Minervini — "free roll" rule.** Corrected from "up ~20% → breakeven" to his
   actual ~+1R sell-half-and-move-stop discipline.
