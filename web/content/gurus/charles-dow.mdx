## Who he was

Charles Dow co-founded Dow Jones & Company and *The Wall Street Journal*. He
never wrote a book on his theory; it was assembled from his editorials after
his death.

## Why he matters

His observations about how markets move in trends became **Dow Theory**, the
conceptual starting point for technical analysis.
