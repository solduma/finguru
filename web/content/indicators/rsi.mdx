## The intuition

RSI asks a simple question: *over the last N periods, how much of the total
price movement was upward?* The answer is squeezed onto a 0–100 scale.

## How to read it

- Above **70**: often called "overbought."
- Below **30**: often called "oversold."

These are starting points, not signals — context decides everything.
